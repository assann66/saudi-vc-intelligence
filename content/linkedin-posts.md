## Post 5: Technical Build Story

**We built a full VC intelligence platform in a day. Here is how.**

The Saudi VC Intelligence Platform — tracking $10.5B across 10 sectors with AI-powered analysis — was built in a single day using AI agents orchestrated by Paperclip.

The stack:
- Next.js 14 (App Router) + TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS with custom glassmorphism dark theme
- Recharts for institutional-grade data visualization
- Claude AI for automated investment analysis and scoring
- Radix UI for accessible, composable components

The build process:

Phase 1 — Data architecture. Modeled 10 sectors and 28 companies with 7-dimensional scoring across investability, risk, growth, funding momentum, competition, market gap, and Vision 2030 relevance.

Phase 2 — AI engine. Connected Claude to generate investment theses, risk assessments, and opportunity scores with full explainability.

Phase 3 — Dashboard. Executive KPI cards, sector radar charts, cumulative funding area charts, sortable company rankings, comparison tools, and deep-dive pages.

Phase 4 — Polish. Animations, responsive design, SEO, loading states. Bloomberg Terminal-inspired aesthetic.

What would have taken a traditional team 2-3 weeks was completed in hours. Not because corners were cut — but because AI agents handled the scaffolding, data modeling, and boilerplate while humans focused on strategy and design decisions.
